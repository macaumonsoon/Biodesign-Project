背景与核心追问 · Background & core questions

Zh：当一个物种消逝，与之相连的生态系统形态也随之瓦解——不仅是生物学意义上的丧失，也是集体记忆中很少被真正看见的共同记忆。我们追问：如何让灭绝物种的「缺席」，成为一种可被感知、可被叙述的「集体记忆中的缺席」？如何借助 AI 与生物数据，温和且有创造力地复苏与重塑对生物多样性、生态完整性的褪色记忆？触觉、视觉、嗅觉、听觉、味觉能否作为轻柔的「记忆线索」，在不过度刺激的前提下，重塑集体记忆并加深共情、责任与共同行动？

En: When a species slips away, an ecosystem pattern goes with it — not only biology, but shared memory that rarely gets witnessed. Q1 How can the “absence” of extinct species become a felt and narratable absence in collective memory? Q2 How can AI and biological data gently revive faded memory of biodiversity and ecological integrity? Q3 How can multisensory cues reshape collective memory and deepen empathy without overstimulation?

我们在做什么 · What are we making?

Zh：我们在搭建行星尺度的数字生物地图档案：当前原型以约 54 个可点击物种纪念点为起点，汇聚末次栖息地、历史分布、灭绝压力、参考文献，以及图像、声音、迁徙与生物节律等感官线索；并可延展至 AR、实体模型、多感官空间与未来活体传感装置。

En: A planetary digital bio-map archive: the prototype begins with 54 clickable memorial points, linking last habitat, historical range, extinction pressure, references, and sensory clues (image, sound, migration, biological rhythm) — expandable toward AR, physical models, multisensory spaces, and living installations.

参观者路径 · How visitors experience it

Zh：

  1. 进入地球仪 → 从行星尺度进入物种纪念点
  2. 打开档案 → 看见物种、地点、时代、压力与文献来源
  3. 感知缺席 → AI 编织声景、旁白、空间与音乐层级
  4. 面对权衡 → 资源、风险、原住民视角与技术边界
  5. 反思与抉择 → 沉浸之后回到判断

En: Enter the globe → open a dossier → sense the absence (soundscape, narration, spatial & music layers) → face resources, risk, Indigenous perspectives, tech limits → reflect and decide.

何以构成「生物设计」· How it becomes biodesign

Zh：生物证据（古生态、节律、迁徙、鸣叫、灭绝驱动）→ 数字化转译（AI 视觉、声景、Web Audio、WebXR、交互档案）→ 反思规则（证据优先，情绪跟随证据；从沉浸回到伦理判断）。

En: Bio evidence (paleo-ecology, rhythm, migration, calls, drivers) → Digital translation (AI visuals, soundscape, Web Audio, WebXR, dossiers) → Reflection rule (evidence first; emotion follows evidence).

核心假设 · Core hypothesis

Zh：若生成式 AI 与 WebXR 能够重构时间表现型（temporal phenotype），我们能否修补人类在感知灭绝时的鸿沟？超越外形：节律、迁徙、声音、脉动与 Umwelt。证据阶梯：古生物数据 + 形态学 + 公开的不确定性分层。

En: If generative AI + WebXR reconstruct a temporal phenotype, can we repair the human gap in sensing extinction? Beyond shape: rhythm, migration, sound, pulse, Umwelt. Evidence ladder: paleo data + morphology + disclosed uncertainty.

为何是「约 54」个物种 · Why this first set is ~54?

Zh：54 不是口号，而是我们能为之道歉、解释并维护的纪念层：49 + 5 —— 49 个高优先级物种加 5 个研究补充；四层对齐（档案、图像、声音、迁徙线索）；可问责——并非包揽全部灭绝叙事，而是第一套可以展示、讲解并持续维护的集合。

En: 54 is not a slogan — it is the memorial layer we can stand behind: 49 + 5 priority & supplement species; four aligned layers (dossier, image, sound, migration); accountable — not all extinctions at once, but the first set we can show, explain, and maintain.

AI 的角色 · What AI does

Zh：连接图像、声音、迁徙、节律与文献来源；标注不确定性而非掩盖；生成声景、旁白与分层音乐；保持提示过程与证据层级可检视。AI 是编目者、作曲者、向导——不是真理的创造者。

En: Connects image, sound, migration, rhythm, and sources; marks uncertainty; generates soundscapes, narration, layered music; keeps prompts & evidence transparent. Cataloger, composer, guide — not creator of truth.

沉浸之后的伦理 · Ethics after immersion

Zh：体验不以情绪收尾，而回到选择与责任。关键权衡包括：资源配置、生态风险、原住民智慧与技术介入边界、以及作为全球性伦理辩论的去灭绝议题。

En: The arc returns to choice and responsibility: resource allocation, ecological risk, Indigenous perspectives, limits of tech intervention, de-extinction as global ethical debate.

核心概念（30 秒版）

Core concept (30-second pitch)

问题：物种灭绝带走的不仅是 DNA，还有其在昼夜与季节中的时间生态位；公众记忆常被压成图像，而非「在世界中的时间」。

Problem: extinction removes not only DNA but temporal niche—daily and seasonal rhythms in the world; memory becomes flat imagery instead of “time-in-the-world.”

方案：WebXR 纪念空间，重建猛犸象与袋狼等案例的节律与感官线索；界面区分 Cited / Interpolated / Speculative，并设伦理分叉与反思环节。

Approach: a WebXR memorial reconstructing rhythms & sensory cues (e.g. mammoth, thylacine); layers tagged Cited / Interpolated / Speculative, plus ethics forks and reflection.
