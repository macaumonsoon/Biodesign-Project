# Extinction Archive / 灭绝档案馆
## AI Memorial for Lost Species / Umwelt Archive
### 感官时间胶囊 / A Sensory Time Capsule

**Biodesign Challenge 2026** / Convergent Life  
Macau University / GUO XIAO YUE / LIU JIA QUN  
Mentor / Atticus SIMS

---

<!-- _class: longcn -->
## 1 Background / 背景

当一个物种悄然灭绝，整个生态系统也随之消失，这不仅带来生物层面的缺失，更在人类社会中造成一种“集体记忆的断裂”。本项目以此为温暖的伦理切入点，邀请大家共同探索一个可亲身体验、值得深入讨论的设计方案。

**EN**  
When a species quietly disappears, an ecosystem pattern disappears with it. The loss is biological, but it also becomes a fracture in human collective memory. This project begins from that ethical and emotional gap.

---

<!-- _class: longcn -->
## 2 Core questions / 核心问题

我们希望通过这个项目，一起思考以下核心问题：
Through this project, we invite the audience to think through three questions:

1. 如何将灭绝物种的“缺失”转化为人类能够切实感知并加以叙述的“集体记忆缺席”？  
   How can extinction become a felt and narratable absence in collective memory?

2. 如何借助人工智能与生物数据，温和而富有创造性地唤起并重塑人类对物种多样性与生态完整性已逐渐淡忘的记忆？  
   How can AI and biological data gently reshape fading memory of biodiversity and ecological integrity?

3. 触觉、视觉、嗅觉、听觉与味觉这些感官，如何能在不造成过度刺激的前提下，作为温柔的“记忆线索”，帮助我们重塑集体记忆，进而唤起更深的共情、责任感与共同行动？
   How can the senses become gentle memory clues without overstimulation?

---

<!-- _class: longcn -->
## 3 What are we making? / 我们要做什么

我们要做的是一个**行星尺度的数字“生物地图档案馆”**：当前原型先呈现 **54 个可点开的物种纪念位**，把已灭绝物种的最后栖息地、历史分布、灭绝压力、参考来源与图像、声音、迁徙、生物节律等感官线索放进同一个可互动档案中；未来可拓展到 **AR、实体模型、多感官空间与活体传感装置**。

We are making a **planetary digital bio-map archive**: the current prototype begins with **54 clickable species memorial points**, bringing together last habitat, historical range, extinction pressure, references, and sensory clues such as image, sound, migration, and biological rhythm; it can expand into **AR, physical models, multisensory spaces, and living sensor installations**.

---

## 4 How visitors experience it / 观众怎么体验

1. **Enter the globe** / 进入地球  
   从行星尺度进入 54 个物种纪念位
   Move from planetary scale into 54 species memorial points

2. **Open a dossier** / 打开档案  
   看见物种、地点、年代、压力与来源
   See species, place, era, pressure, and sources

3. **Sense the absence** / 感知缺席  
   AI 生成声景、叙述、360° 环境与音乐层
   AI builds soundscape, narration, 360° space, and music layers

4. **Reflect and decide** / 反思与选择  
   面对资源、风险、原住民视角与技术边界
   Face resources, risk, Indigenous perspectives, and tech limits

---

## 5 How it becomes “biodesign” / 生物证据 + 数字体验

**Bio evidence / 生物证据**  
古生态资料、节律、迁徙、叫声、灭绝原因  
Paleo-ecology, rhythm, migration, calls, extinction drivers

**Digital translation / 数字转译**  
AI 影像、声景、Web Audio、WebXR、互动档案  
AI visuals, soundscape, Web Audio, WebXR, interactive dossiers

**Reflection / 反思出口**  
从沉浸体验回到伦理判断  
From immersion back to ethical judgment

**Rule / 原则**  
证据在前，情感跟随证据。  
Evidence first. Emotion follows evidence.

---

## 6 Core hypothesis / 不只复原外形，而是复原时间感

**If generative AI + WebXR reconstruct a temporal phenotype, can we repair the human gap in sensing extinction?**

如果用生成式 AI 与 WebXR 重建一种**时间中的表型**，能否补上人类在感知灭绝时的缺口？

**Temporal phenotype / 时间中的表型**  
外形之外，还有节律、迁徙、声音、心率与环境感知。  
Beyond shape: rhythm, migration, sound, pulse, Umwelt.

**Evidence ladder / 证据阶梯**  
古生态资料 + 形态线索 + 可说明的不确定性  
Paleo data + morphology + disclosed uncertainty

---

## 7 Scope / 第一批纪念范围

**我们先做 54 个可负责的物种纪念位。**  
**We begin with 54 accountable species memorial points.**

1. **来源**：49 个高优先级物种 + 5 个研究补充物种。  
   **Source**: 49 high-priority species + 5 research supplements.

2. **对齐**：档案、图像、声音、迁徙线索进入同一体验层。  
   **Alignment**: dossier, image, sound, and migration clues enter one experience layer.

3. **边界**：不是全部灭绝史，而是第一批可展示、可解释、可维护的范围。  
   **Boundary**: not all extinctions, but the first set we can show, explain, and maintain.

---

## 8 Method / AI 如何把档案变成体验

**AI 不是制造事实，而是把资料转译成可感体验。**  
**AI does not create truth. It translates records into experience.**

1. **连接**：图像、声音、迁徙、节律与来源。  
   **Connect**: image, sound, migration, rhythm, and sources.

2. **生成**：声景、叙述与层叠音乐。  
   **Generate**: soundscapes, narration, and layered music.

3. **标记**：证据、推测与不确定性。  
   **Mark**: evidence, speculation, and uncertainty.

---

## 9 Exit / 沉浸之后进入伦理判断

**体验不止于感动，最后要回到选择与责任。**  
**The experience does not end with emotion. It returns to choice and responsibility.**

1. **资源与风险** / Resources and ecological risk
2. **原住民智慧与视角** / Indigenous wisdom and perspectives
3. **技术介入边界** / Limits of technological intervention
4. **去灭绝讨论** / De-extinction as ethical debate

纪念不是占有名字。
Memorial is not possession.
